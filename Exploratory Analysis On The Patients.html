For all the cases we have in the dataset, the average generalization of each seizure starts at 86 seconds inside the seizure.

We observe that a seizure, if generalized, will do so towards the end of the time period for which it lasts. 86 seconds (1 minute 43 seconds), since a seizure lasts from 30 seconds to 2 minutes, is the average for the seizures we have, so it really generalizes the seizure towards the end.
